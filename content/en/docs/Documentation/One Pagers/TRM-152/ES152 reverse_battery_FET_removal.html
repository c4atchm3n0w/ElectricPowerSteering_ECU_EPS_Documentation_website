Remove Reverse-Battery Protection FET from EPS Control Modules

  • Description 

    • Remove the reverse-battery protection FET from the EPS control module, and replace with a short circuit 

    • Add local reverse-battery protection (diodes) at power supply and gate drive 

    • For a reverse-battery condition, depend on the vehicle fuse to blow before an inverter FET is damaged 

  • Risk: Inverter FET Damage 

    • Fuses have huge variation in performance 

      • An MTA MIDI fuse at 200% of current rating is guaranteed to blow somewhere between 3s and 100s 

      • An MTA MIDI fuse at 500% of current rating is guaranteed to blow somewhere between  0.1s and 1.0s 

    • Testing has shown that we can not depend on equal current sharing among the 3 legs of the inverter
