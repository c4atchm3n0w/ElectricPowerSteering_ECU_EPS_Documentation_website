May 7, 2014 Update

    • Increase the Max Absolute Vernier Treshold Error  (9.5 degrees  => 10.0 degrees) based on simulation of the filtering taking place in controller .  10.0 + 4.5 (Temp. allocation) = 14.5 degrees Controller limit.

    • Re-Running the Probe Housings at the station
